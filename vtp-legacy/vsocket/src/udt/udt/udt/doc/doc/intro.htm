UDT: UDP-Based Data Transfer Library

Welcome to the UDT SDK documentation.

UDT is also the name of our high performance data transfer protocol - UDP-based data transfer protocol. It is designed for data intensive applications over wide area networks such as computational grids, to overcome the efficiency and fairness problems of TCP.

 

Check out most recent UDT release at SourceForge or from CVS.

export CVS_RSH=ssh

cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/dataspace login

cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/dataspace co UDT
